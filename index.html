My Hobbies

Hello! Im Toni Bedassie, a passionate 15-year-old artist and student currently navigating my secondary school journey. My creativity spans across various mediums, including traditional and digital art, where I express myself through graphic design, acrylic and watercolor painting, sketching, and miniature art. I also enjoy crafting unique paper art pieces.In addition to visual arts, I delve into the world of music production, creating relaxing lofi beats that complement my artistic vibe. As a poet, I love weaving words together to evoke emotions and tell stories. Photography is another hobby of mine, allowing me to capture the beauty around me and inspire my artwork. I believe that art is a powerful form of communication, relief and a mix of emotions and tones. I strive to convey emotions and stories through my creations. Im excited to share my work and connect with fellow art enthusiasts! Thank you for visitingmyportfolio!
